This page separates author tasks from the homepage. It keeps every author-facing fact in HTML text so authors, search engines, and AI retrieval systems can read the same content.

Submission Requirements Peer review Templates APF

Submission path

  • Submit manuscripts through the IJICS online submission system.
  • Register and log in through the official IJICS submission login.
  • DOC or PDF files can be accepted; PDF files are preferred.
  • The submitting author should confirm the upload and complete co-author list.
  • The author receives an email acknowledgment and reference number after successful reception.
  • Review status can be inquired about by email.
Submission login Open ijics.cn/user/login

Manuscript requirements

  • Submitted manuscripts should not have been published or be under submission elsewhere without written consent.
  • Contributions should reflect recent developments, academic novelty, thoughtfulness, and practicability.
  • The manuscript should use a two-column format.
  • Writing should be simple and straightforward, with reliable data and no undue self-appraisal.
  • Low-quality manuscripts, poor English writing, or tedious quotations may be rejected without review.

Peer review and screening

IJICS states that articles are peer reviewed by a minimum of two independent reviewers through a single-blind review process.

Overall similarity < 30%
Single-source similarity < 15%
Author self-citations < 20%
References from one source < 20%

Article Publish Fee

No manuscript processing fee is currently charged

The current IJICS page states that the journal does not charge any fees for manuscript processing. It also states that any future article publish fee will be made visible on the journal website.
