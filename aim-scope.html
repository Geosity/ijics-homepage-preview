Journal objective

IJICS publishes articles describing recent fundamental contributions and innovative applications in intelligent control and systems. Its objective is to disseminate important and leading-edge information in this field in a timely fashion.

Theoretical contributions

The journal covers theoretical contributions aimed at further understanding of intelligent technologies and knowledge-based systems, including robotics and automation, manufacturing, intelligent machines, design methodologies, modeling and simulation, system architectures, learning and reasoning, multisensor fusion, and intelligent control.

Practical contributions

Practical contributions include advances in hardware and software development environments for intelligent technologies and knowledge-based systems, including smart chips, intelligent devices and instruments, real-time architectures, rapid prototyping, intelligent human-machine interfaces, simulation software, and CAD/CAM tools.
